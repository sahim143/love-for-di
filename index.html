To My Dearest Di ❤️

From Your Da – Sachin Gowda

My Dearest Himarashmi,

Every day with you feels like a beautiful dream that I never want to wake up from. Your smile lights up my darkest days, your voice calms every storm inside me, and your love has become the most precious part of my life.

No matter where life takes us, I promise to stand beside you, hold your hand through every happiness and every challenge, and love you with all my heart.

You are my peace, my happiness, my best friend, and the greatest blessing I have ever received.

I don't need the whole world because my world begins and ends with you.

I love you today, tomorrow, forever, and beyond. ❤️ Forever Yours ❤️

— Your Da 💖
💖 I Love You Infinite Times, Di 💖
